You are an occupant in a smart home that has an automatic light system installed. The lights can turn on and off when you are doing certain activities.

The smart home can only recognize commands to turn the lights on or off.

Please create 5 commands that you would say to the house to tell it whether the lights should be on or off when you are performing certain activities. Include the activity you are performing in the command.

Activities the smart home recognizes:

  • Bathing
  • Going from the bed to the toilet
  • Eating
  • Entering home
  • Housekeeping
  • Leaving home
  • Preparing meals
  • Personal hygiene
  • Sleeping (in bed)
  • Sleeping (not in bed)
  • Wandering in room
  • Watch TV
  • Work

Examples of commands (please do not use these examples):

  • Turn off the lights in the living room since I’m watching TV.
  • I’m going to bed now, so turn off the lights.
  • When I’m leaving home, turn off the lights.
  • Turn on the lights when I’m in the kitchen.
  • Turn on the lights, I’m brushing my teeth.
